Schools Out

Out of hours childcare for primary school children

About Us

We offer a professional ‘Out of School Hours’ Service and Sports programme to primary school children.

Our afterschool and sports programme is built on having fun and play, ensuring that the atmosphere is different to that in a classroom.

Our aim is to extend children’s daily learning by involving them in a range of creative and sport programmes that will positively stimulate them physically, cognitively, socially and emotionally.

Services

After School Club

Our afterschool club takes place when classes finish and continues up to 6.30pm. Our aim is to extend your child’s daily learning by involving them in fun activities that will positively challenge and stimulate them both physically and cognitively.

Your child can unwind in a relaxed, safe and fun, home from home environment which is part of the school community. Our after school and sports model plays an important role in the social and emotional development of children and is not intended to be an extension of the school day.

Holiday Camps

At Schools out camps we offer a full range of fun and exciting weekly activities and themes to ensure children are entertained, making friends and most importantly having fun! They can learn to develop new skills and interests in a fun and challenging environment and choose from a whole variety of exciting activities including: Football, Athletics, Tennis, Basketball, Rounders, Yoga, Art Projects, Crafts, Cooking, Quizzes, Science Experiments, Indoor and Outdoor games, GAA, Music, Dance and much more.

Our camps are held during school holidays including: Summer holidays: Weekly camps from 2nd July to 24th August – 8.30am – 6:30pm October mid-term – 8.30am – 6:30pm Christmas break – 8:30am – 6:30pm February Mid-Term – 8.30am – 6:30pm Easter break – 8.30am – 6:30pm May Bank Holiday 8:30am – 6:30pm
