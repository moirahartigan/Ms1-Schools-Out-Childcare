Our Philosophy

We believe that children coming from school need to come into a very relaxed, comfortable and less structured environment. The afterschool setting should be an environment where the children can be themselves, develop friendships, discover and follow their own interests and a place where they feel cared for and cared about.

Our afterschool service recognises the huge potential of the afterschool age child. We believes that children have a basic human right to play and that play is central to the well-being and development of children.

It recognises too, that children have a natural instinct to learn, explore and discover and need to be given the time and space to do just that.

Our afterschool service believes in taking a child centred and holistic approach that strives to provide daily routines and experiences which are fun and encourage the physical, social, emotional, intellectual, creative, cultural and linguistic development of the children who attend the service.

We believe that homework should be supported and facilitated but should not be the core activity of the Afterschool Care Service.

Our goal is for all children to develop high self-esteem, to reach their individual potential and to always feel included, loved and successful.
